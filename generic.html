History about Eupho

Short story, the birth of this site came from a small project that I made for homework purposes. Since I've lacked any coding experience up to this point, I took a pre-existing design from HTML5UP and tweaked some bits. The site took 3 weeks to make, this includes writing the reviews and a lot of trial-and-error.

The "anime reviews" idea is kind of inspired by MyAnimeList, an anime database website. You can basically make your list of animes you planned to watch, dropped, completed, etc. I've been making a list of animes that I've watched so far, so instead of putting it to waste, this is the easiest idea I could think off. I could promote a merchandise or something, but I'm not interested.

About the domain stuff, I think I will do it at the time I present the work later, after that however, it depends. This site is supposed to be small-scale anyways so hopefully with that in mind, I can find something that didn't cost my entire budget.
