Dandadan

I heard so many good things from Dandadan, particularly the manga with it's dynamic fight scenes being praised more than often. When the anime was announced back in 2024 and then the first PV was dropped, the moment I saw the studio Science Saru, I knew something's cooking. These guys are the same heads behind Ping Pong the Animation, Heike Monogatari, which are some of the best display of direction and animation-wise in recent years. After it came out, I didn't had time to watch the anime itself until recently, and oh boy, the experience is wild.
